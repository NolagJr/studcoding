Install Plugin
Get Coraic running in Roblox Studio in under 2 minutes.
1
Download the plugin file
Click below to download plugin.lua.
Download plugin.lua
2
Open your Plugins folder
Press Win + R, paste this and hit Enter:
%localappdata%\Roblox\Plugins
3
Drop file in, restart Studio
Copy plugin.lua into that folder, fully close and reopen Studio. The Coraic button appears in your toolbar.
4
Enter your Plugin Key
Open Coraic in Studio and paste your plugin key. Find yours in Account above.

About Coraic
What we're building and why.
Stop debugging. Start shipping.
Coraic is an AI-powered platform built for Roblox developers. We fix your scripts directly inside Studio — zero copy-pasting — and teach you scripting through lessons and quizzes.
Join the Coraic Community
Get help, share your games, talk to other Roblox developers.
Send Feedback
Bug, suggestion, or just want to say something?
Message us on Discord
Why we built this
Debugging Roblox Lua is genuinely painful, especially when you're learning. We built Coraic to live inside Studio, know what RemoteEvents are, and explain errors without sounding like a textbook.
