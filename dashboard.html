Install Plugin
Get Coraic running inside Roblox Studio in under 2 minutes.
1
Download the plugin file
Click below to download plugin.lua — the Coraic Studio plugin.
Download plugin.lua
2
Open your Plugins folder
Press Win + R, paste this and hit Enter:
%localappdata%\Roblox\Plugins
3
Drop the file in, restart Studio
Copy plugin.lua into that Plugins folder. Fully close and reopen Roblox Studio. The Coraic button will appear in your toolbar.
4
Enter your Plugin Key
Open Coraic in Studio and paste your plugin key. Find yours in Account above.

About Coraic
What we're building and why.
Stop debugging. Start shipping.
Coraic is an AI-powered platform built for Roblox developers. We fix your scripts directly inside Studio — zero copy-pasting — and teach you scripting through lessons and quizzes that don't sound like a textbook.
Join the Coraic Community
Get help, share your games, talk to other Roblox developers.
Send Feedback
Bug, suggestion, or just want to say something? We read everything.
Or message us on Discord
Why we built this
Debugging Roblox Lua is genuinely painful, especially when you're learning. Most tutorials stop at the basics. AI tools don't understand Roblox's quirks. We built Coraic to live inside Studio, know what RemoteEvents are, and explain errors without sounding like a textbook.
