// How to Use
Get Started with Stud
Get up and running in under 5 minutes.
1
Create your free account
Sign up at studcoding.app with your email. Takes 30 seconds.
2
Get your Plugin Key
Go to Get Plugin in the sidebar. Click Generate Key to get your unique SC-XXXXX-XXXXX key.
3
Install the Studio Plugin
Click Open in Roblox Studio on the Get Plugin page. The plugin installs directly from the Roblox Creator Store — no file copying needed.
4
Connect in Roblox Studio
Open Studio and click the StudCoding button in your plugins toolbar. Paste your SC key, hit Connect, then enter the 6-digit code sent to your email. You're in — session saves for 30 days.
5
Fix, Write, and Learn
Use the Fix tab to repair broken scripts, the Write tab to generate new ones, and come back here to use the AI Tutor, Lessons, and Quiz to level up your Lua skills.
