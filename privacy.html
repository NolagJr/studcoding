Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 15, 2026

Coraic ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Coraic platform, including our website, dashboard, and Roblox Studio plugin.

By using Coraic, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you are under 18, your parent or guardian must review and agree to this policy on your behalf.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account and use Coraic:

  • Account information: Your username, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash — we never store your plain-text password)
  • Usage data: Features you use, lessons completed, quiz scores, and AI chat history
  • Plugin activity: Scripts submitted for fixing or writing through the Roblox Studio plugin
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information for security and abuse prevention
  • Cookies: Session tokens to keep you logged in, and optional analytics cookies (with your consent)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the Coraic platform
  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To send you email verification codes and important account notices
  • To personalize your learning experience based on quiz and lesson history
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and security threats
  • To analyze platform usage and improve our product (only with your consent)

3. Cookies

We use three categories of cookies:

  • Necessary cookies: Required for the platform to function. These include your session token that keeps you logged in. You cannot opt out of these.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with Coraic. We only use these with your explicit consent.
  • Marketing cookies: Used for ad campaign tracking. We only use these with your explicit consent.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Anthropic: Your AI chat messages are processed by Claude AI (Anthropic's API) to generate responses. Anthropic's privacy policy governs their use of this data.
  • Railway: Our hosting provider stores your account data on their servers.
  • Resend: We use Resend to deliver verification emails. Only your email address is shared.
  • Law enforcement: We may disclose information if required by Canadian law or a valid court order.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us at legal@coraic.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Coraic is used by developers of all ages, including those under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you are under 13, please have a parent or guardian create and manage your account. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13 without consent, contact us immediately at legal@coraic.com.

7. Your Rights (PIPEDA — Canada)

Under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

To exercise any of these rights, email us at legal@coraic.com.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including password hashing, JWT authentication, HTTPS encryption, and rate limiting on all authentication routes. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on the platform. Continued use of Coraic after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

  • Email: legal@coraic.com
  • Website: coraic.com

This Privacy Policy was last updated on March 15, 2026 and applies to all users of the Coraic platform.
